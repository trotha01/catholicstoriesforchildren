Start teaching your children with Catholic animations

Use these animations to help your kids build a habit of prayer.

Do you want your children to grow in gratitude? Start with the prayer before meals.Do you want your children to grow in humility? Start with the Act of Contrition.Do you want your children to grow in love and charity? Start incorporating intentions for other people into your prayers.
